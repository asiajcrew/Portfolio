About Alina

Alina Baraz is a singer & songwriter from Cleveland, Ohio. She rose to fame in 2015 after releasing her first album, Urban Flora. Since then Alina has gone on to release two other albums, The Color of You and It Was Divine. You can listen to Baraz's music on all music streaming platforms including Apple Music.
